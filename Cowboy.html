CowBoy Boy



CowBoy Boy is a fast paced, platforming, experimental, local multiplayer shooter featuring some cowboy boys! PC CONTROLS:

Space - Shoot
WASD - Player1 movement and jump
IJKL - Players2 movement and jump
ARROW KEYS - Player3 movement and jump

XBOX CONTROLLER CONTROLS

Y - Jump
A - Shoot
Left stick - Player movement and jump
